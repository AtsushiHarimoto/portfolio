## 1. The Image Generation Engine: Checkpoint Models

Commonly known as the "Base Model." It determines whether your output is a refined Japanese anime style or a hyper-realistic photograph.

- **SDXL (The Value King)**:
  The most stable "Big Brother" in the ecosystem. It's rich in support and, importantly: **it's not picky about your GPU!** If your VRAM is limited to 8GB or 12GB, this is your best bet. `Animagine XL 4.0` is the currently undisputed "heaven-tier" for anime character generation.
- **Flux (The Next-Gen Visual Monster)**:
  The dark horse of 2025. Its image quality is beyond words, even solving the age-old AI struggle of getting text and letters right!
  - **The Catch**: It's a resource hog. Without 24GB of VRAM (RTX 3090/4090), it runs as slow as dial-up internet. If your memory is tight, look for compressed versions labeled **`GGUF`** or **`FP8`**—the savior for budget setups.

> 📁 **Supervisor's Storage Rule**: These checkpoints must be stored at: `ComfyUI/models/checkpoints/`.

---

## 2. Injected Soul: LoRA Models (Small Supplements)

If you want the AI to precisely draw a specific character (like Reimu from "Touhou Project") or follow a specific "Marble Texture Style," you don't need to change the base model; you just mount a **LoRA.** These are only a few hundred MBs—like a "recipe pill" for the chef, teaching them a specific specialty instantly.

- **Usage**: Hot-swappable. Switch from an elegant Cheongsam to Cyberpunk armor in seconds.
- 📁 **Supervisor's Storage Rule**: Store them at: `ComfyUI/models/loras/`.

---

## 3. Breathing Life into Static Art: Video Generation (SVD & WAN)

Who watches static images anymore? We want breathing, blinking life! Moyin's core pipeline is now fully integrated with the **WAN 2.1 / 2.2** series released by Alibaba.

- **I2V (Image to Video)**: Feed a character image, and it returns a 5-second clip of her tossing her hair.
- **T2V (Text to Video)**: Say a word, and it conjures a cinematic movie scene from thin air.

---

## 4. Black Magic for the Ears: Voice Cloning (TTS)

An NPC without a voice is an NPC without a soul. we use two cutting-edge technologies:

1.  **GPT-SoVITS**: **The World's Best Zero-Shot Voice Cloning.**
    This is insane. Give me just 5 seconds of your recording, and the AI will instantly "capture your frequency and resonance." Next time, the NPC talks exactly like you.
2.  **IndexTTS2**: A premium choice for nuance.
    It allows fine control over "emotional labels," such as making the heroine speak with a "Tsundere" or "Melancholic" tone—the true essence of a visual novel.
