# The Iron Claws of Vibe-Coding: BMAD-Lite Lifecycle Guard (Vibe Coding Rules)

## @Overview

"Vibe-Coding" is not about coding on a whim. It is a high-level development methodology where AI and humans share a "common discipline," merging intuition with a rigorous process.

In the Moyin project, to prevent AI from going rogue or rewriting code without a design, we have implemented a strict gatekeeping system called **BMAD-Lite (Lifecycle Guard)**.

---

## 1. The Six Phases of Development (Absolute Order)

No matter how trivial the fix, skipping the following steps is strictly forbidden:

1.  **DOC CHECK (Pre-verification of Docs & Specs)**
    - Thoroughly investigate if there are any contradictions with existing design documents.
    - Clarify whether the "document is outdated" or the "code is wrong."
2.  **PLAN (Presentation of Concrete Execution Plan)**
    - Output a structured document including impact scope, technical risks, and test strategy.
3.  **APPROVAL (Human Authorization)**
    - The system must pause; AI shall not save a single character until the human says `OK`.
4.  **ACTION (Implementation based on Plan)**
    - Perform precise coding only within the scope of the approved plan.
5.  **VERIFY (Verification with Evidence)**
    - Execute tests and submit objective evidence (logs, snapshots, etc.).
6.  **POST DOC CHECK (Reverse Spec Alignment)**
    - Re-check the implemented code against the original documents and update documents if necessary.

---

## 2. Guarding UI/UX Fidelity

In frontend development, it is strictly forbidden for the AI to "take initiative based on subjective aesthetic judgment."

- **No Doc, No UI**: Any addition or deletion of visual elements is only legal if documented in the Design Spec. "Personal perfection is the greatest enemy of precise specifications."
- **Design Double-Check**: Before and after action, the AI must explicitly check which section of the design guidelines the components belong to.

---

## 3. Component & Style Reuse (Reuse First)

We strictly prevent "reinventing the wheel."

- **Lookup Order**: Check `components/ui/*` (shared UI) ➝ `theme.css` (Design Tokens) first. 100% utilization of existing assets is mandatory.
- **Token Enforcement**: Hardcoding a "primary color" as `#ff0000` is a cardinal sin. System variables like `var(--ui-primary)` must always be used.

---

## 💡 Vibecoding Pro-Tip for Discipline

To make the AI follow this lifecycle guard, declare it clearly:

> 🗣️ `“AI Assistant! Listen up! For this session, the [BMAD-Lite] Lifecycle Guard is in full effect!
>
> 1. Start with a DOC CHECK to identify any spec conflicts.
> 2. Then, output a structured PLAN including impact and risks, and wait for my OK.
> 3. After implementation, you must VERIFY with evidence (logs/screenshots).
>    Do not cut corners or skip steps. Go!”`

This discipline is the only key to balancing "speed" and "quality" in AI-driven development.
