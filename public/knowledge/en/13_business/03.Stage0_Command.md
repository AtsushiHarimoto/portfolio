# Stage 0 Execution Manual: 4-Week Market Validation Command (Stage 0 Command)

## @Overview

This manual translates the high-level business strategic blueprint into a daily executable operations handbook. Our core principles are: minimize initial burnout, ruthlessly eliminate branching, establish sample size guardrails, and implement rigorous asset compliance.

---

## 0. Unified Narrative (Cross-Channel Specification)

> **"A character-driven short interactive story demo (5–10 minutes), designed to validate the narrative hooks and market resonance of the characters."**

All store copy, trailers, short videos, Reddit promotions, and community engagement must serve this central thesis.

---

## 1. Defining "Victory" for Stage 0 (Non-Monetary)

**Objective: Utilize a 4-week window to gather decision-making data on whether it is worth proceeding with an "Early Access (EA)" build.**
Stage 0 does not focus on revenue forecasting or meeting a 2,000 wishlist target; instead, it focuses exclusively on: **WVR (Wishlist/Visits Rate), Completion Rate, Negative Feedback Structure, and Community Interaction.**

---

## 2. Scope Boundaries (Strict Budgeting: Max 80 Hours)

**The "Do Not Do" List (Explicitly Excluded from Stage 0):**

- [ ] Complex narrative branching or multiple endings.
- [ ] Full-text voiceovers (retain only 3–5 emotionally charged key lines).
- [ ] Live-Generated systems (excluded from the Steam release roadmap).
- [ ] Custom landing pages or exhaustive world-building documents (one-page link tree only).

**The "Must Do" List (The Critical Core of Stage 0):**

- [ ] **3–5 Minute Single Loop**: Ensure a seamless flow from introduction to credits without softlocks.
- [ ] **Steam Page Launch**: Utilize placeholder screenshots if necessary, provided they maintain thematic consistency.
- [ ] **itch.io Deployment**: Full fulfillment of AI Disclosure (Generative AI Statement) requirements.
- [ ] **Tier 1 Asset Registry**: Establish a minimal, one-click exportable compliance package (ZIP).
- [ ] **500 Unique Visits Threshold**: No conclusions are to be drawn unless a minimum of 500 visits is achieved.

---

## 3. KPI v3: Two-Tier Decision Matrix

> **Sample Guardrail**: WVR data is only statistically significant once Unique Visits ≥ 500.

### A) Steam: WVR (Wishlist / Visits)

- **Go (Proceed to Q2)**: WVR ≥ 5% AND Visits ≥ 500.
- **Pivot (Refit & Retest)**: WVR 3–5% with actionable qualitative feedback (e.g., slow pacing).
- **Switch (Plan B Pivot)**: WVR < 3% with 500+ visits.

### B) itch.io: Completion Rate (Proximal Feedback)

- **Evaluation Method**: Post-credits popup → Link to Google Form (Question 1: Did you reach the end?).
- **Metrics (Submissions / Downloads)**:
  - **Go**: ≥ 30%
  - **Pivot**: 20–30%
  - **Switch**: < 20% (unless caused by technical bugs).

### C) Negative Feedback (The "Soul" Redline)

Even if WVR is high, if feedback focuses on:

- "Too much AI-generated scent / Hollow narrative / Lack of soul/emotional weight."
  - → This indicates a fundamental failure in narrative direction. **Action**: Transition to a high human-intervention model (80/20 manual refinement) rather than more AI generation.

## 4. The 4-Week Sprint Timeline (Budget: 80-Hour Budget)

- **Week 1 (20h): Store Infrastructure First**
  - Synthesize 3 core character sprites (reusable across store/shorts), draft Steam page (copy/capsule art/5 placeholder screens).
- **Week 2 (20h): MVP Implementation + itch.io Launch**
  - Deploy the 3–5 min single loop, integrate the Google Form feedback entry, execute itch.io upload with AI disclosure.
- **Week 3 (20h): Steam Review + Minimal Marketing**
  - Submit Steam Demo for review (Pre-Generated AI declaration), post 4 short videos (3 character hooks + 1 founder vision), activate the Discord server.
- **Week 4 (20h): Data Distillation + Decision定居**
  - Daily data tracking (WVR/Visits/Source analysis), final Go / Pivot / Switch decision at EOW4.
