# Moyin: Industrial AI Interactive Engine Business Strategic Blueprint (2026)

## @Overview

Project Moyin is strategically pivoting from a simple "AI-assisted creation tool" into a next-generation **"AI-Native Interactive Content Factory."** This blueprint outlines our industrial-grade production pipeline, market positioning, economic models, and compliance strategies for the 2026 AI entertainment landscape.

---

## 1. Lean Canvas Analysis

### Market Opportunity & Problems

- **The Problem**: Traditional animation/game production is cost-prohibitive ($50k+/min) with long lead times. Modern social media requires "daily/weekly" content updates which legacy pipelines cannot support.
- **The Solution (Moyin Engine)**: An industrial-grade production line based on ComfyUI + Rust. We deliver "AI-Native Galgames" (Visual Novels) that compress production costs while maintaining high narrative engagement.

### Unique Value Proposition (UVP)

- **"Tokyo Compliance"**: Operating within the Japanese legal framework to provide "traceable, replaceable, and auditable" AI content, addressing the industry's largest risk: platform delisting.
- **"Alive Character"**: Focused on emotional performance via Director Agents rather than static AI-generated imagery.

---

## 2. Unit Economics (Double-Unit Model)

As the product shifts from pure animation to interactive visual novels, the core cost driver is no longer GPU compute or electricity, but **"Founder / Engineering Time."**

### Unit A: Asset Production Cost (P90 Estimates)

| Asset Type                        | GPU Time | Engineering/Human-in-the-Loop | Total Cost (P90) |
| :-------------------------------- | :------- | :---------------------------- | :--------------- |
| **Key CG (1 Image)**              | 15 min   | 0.75 hours                    | ~$23             |
| **Character Sprite (3 Variants)** | 30 min   | 1.5 hours                     | ~$45             |
| **Voiceover (1 min)**             | 5 min    | 0.5 hours                     | ~$15             |
| **1 Scene (Script + Logic)**      | -        | 3.0 hours                     | ~$90             |

- **Strategic Conclusion**: Cash burn on electricity is negligible (< $0.15); the real bottleneck is human oversight. Scaling requires high asset reuse and automated quality insurance (QA).

---

## 3. Revenue Matrix & Monetization

### Three-Stage Rocket Model

1.  **Level 1 (Top-of-Funnel)**: Viral short-form content on TikTok/Shorts. Used for zero-cost user acquisition and testing IP resonance.
2.  **Level 2 (Retention & Community)**: Discord-based engagement and Patreon subscriptions for "Early Access" and behind-the-scenes content.
3.  **Level 3 (Monetization)**: Full game releases on Steam/DLsite ($15-$25 price point). This is the final stage of IP value extraction.

---

## 4. Legal Risk & Compliance Framework

Moyin utilizes the relatively clear Japanese policy framework to manage AI copyright risks while ensuring global distribution compatibility.

- **Japan Market**: Adhering to Copyright Act Article 30-4, focusing on non-consumptive information analysis for machine learning.
- **Platform Compliance**:
  - **Steam**: Full disclosure of "AI-Generated Assets" following the latest Content Survey requirements.
  - **DLsite**: Strict adherence to "AI-generated category" rules and avoiding LoRAs that mimic specific living artists.
- **Traceability (Asset Registry)**: Maintaining a comprehensive log for every asset, including prompt strings, model versions, seed values, and manual edit history.

  - Epic-003: Finalizing the minimal closed-loop pipeline (Director Agent + Asset Factory).
  - Market Validation: Releasing a 5-10 minute playable loop to measure Wishlist/Visit Rate (WVR).

---

## 6. Critical Risk Analysis

- **Consumer Backlash**: Hostility toward AI content in core gaming communities.
  - **Mitigation**: Position AI as a "Production Tool" rather than the "Feature." Emphasize human-written narratives and high-fidelity manual refinement (the 80/20 rule).
- **Platform Dependency**: Sudden changes in AI policies by Steam/DLsite.
  - **Mitigation**: Multi-platform distribution and a "Plan B" pivot toward a B2B "Compliance Toolkit" for other independent developers.
